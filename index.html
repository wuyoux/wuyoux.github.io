About Me:


Hello!
My name is William Xie and I am a sophomore at the University of Texas at Austin. Currently, I study mathematics with a strong interest in statistics and its applications in financial modeling. Specifically, I would like to learn more about statistical projection methods and their applications in the quant trading field. While generally, my ambition is to base trading decisions on statistical models, I still perform and learn about traditional analysis methods and participate in stock pitch competitions. Outside of finance, I enjoy teaching mathematics and physics, and will rarely pass up an opportunity to tutor. Presently, I am an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant for a Waves and Oscillations class. Away from academia, I keep a pretty strict workout schedule, love good music, and am particularly interested in engineering devices to solve problems. I look forward to meeting you!
